The Types of Insurance

Typically provided by employers and chosen by you. You do not have to elect for every type of insurance an employer offers.

Health Insurance

Essential to support you through health complications, emergencies, sicknesses, and preventative care.

Dental Insurance

Covers routine cleaning and dental proceures

Vision Insurance

Covers eye care, eye exams, and visual aids.

Life Insurance

Provdies financial support to your designated beneficiaries if you were to pass away. Many employers offer a non-voluntary life insurance which they incur the cost of.

Homeowner's Insurance

This insurance covers damage your home may incur during storms, fires, or theft.

Renter's Insurance

Covers personal belongings on a rented property, whether they be stolen or otherwise damaged.

Auto Insurance

Required by law in many places. Protects against car damages and personal injuries during accidents.

Disability Insurance

Helps you still make some income in the event of an illness or injury putting you out of work.

Pet Insurance

Covers verterinary bills for your pets.
