The Goal

The ultimate goal of financial literacy education is to provide you with the knowledge and skills to make smart decisions with your financial resources.

The following pages will show you the complete basics of personal finance. Everyone deals with these aspects of personal finance in their adult life. These may not be used daily, or be something you consistently think about, but when you run into them, you'll be glad to have learned about them.
