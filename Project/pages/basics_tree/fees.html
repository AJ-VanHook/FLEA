Maintenance Fees

These fees are charged monthly for maintaining your account. Sometimes these fees are waived if you attain a minimum balance, have a direct deposit, or if the institution offers a fee-free type of account.

Statement Fees

Some institutions charge a fee for sending you paper bank statements in the mail.

Overdraft Fees

If you spend more than your available balance, your bank will likely charge an overdraft fee and cover the difference.

Returned Deposit Fees

If you deposit a check that bounces, you will be charged a returned deposit fee.

Stop Payment Fees

If you write a check that needs to be stopped for any reason, you can contact your bank to stop the payment. They will charge a stop payment fee.

ATM Fees

If you withdraw money that is out of your bank's ATM network, you will be charged a small fee.

Transfer Fees

If you transfer money to a different account at a different banking institution, you will be charged a transfer fee. This fee is sometimes a percentage or sometimes fixed.

Excess Transaction Fees

This fee can be charged if you make to many transactions to an account with a transaction limit, usually a savings account.
