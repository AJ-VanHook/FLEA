Debit Vs. Credit

Return to Main Page Back

Debit

By using debit, usually with a debit card or by writing a check, you are spending money you already have earned.

Credit

By using credit, usually with a credit card, you are incuring debt that you promise to repay later, typically monthly.

If you do not repay the charge within a specified period of time, you will be charged interest, usually at a high rate. This is how credit card companies make money by allowing you to spend their money.
