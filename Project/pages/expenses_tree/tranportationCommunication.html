Car Payments

Purchasing a vehicle is an expense that many choose to do in cash. It is much more reasonable to purchase a vehicle in full than a home, and it keeps you out of debt. Otherwise, when you purchase a vehicle, you will typically take out a loan. This loan will accure interest over time and some car loans can get very pricey very quickly, so it's a good idea to get off this debt as quickly as possible.
