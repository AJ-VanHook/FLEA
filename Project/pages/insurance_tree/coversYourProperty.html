Insurance That Covers Your Property

Homeowner's Insurance

Homeowner's insurance is a type of insurance that covers damage your home may incur during storms, fires, or theft.

Renter's Insurance

Renter's insurance provides coverage for losses or damage to your property in the event of an accident when you are living on or in a rental unit. Two-thirds of American renter's do not have renter's insurance.

Auto Insurance

This insurance is required by law in most of the United States. There are two main types of auto insurance: collision and comprehensive.

Collision coverage covers you when there is vehicle damage due to an accident. Comprehensive coverage covers damage to your vehicle not caused by an accident.
