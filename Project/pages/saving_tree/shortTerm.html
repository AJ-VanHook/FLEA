What Constitues a Short Term Goal?

A short-term goal is a goal that can be acheived in 1 to 3 years. These goals are to help you in the present or into the near future. Financial short-term goals typically improve your quality of life and mitigate the risk of going broke.

Examples of Short Term Goals

  • Emergency fund saving
  • Purchasing a car
  • Paying off a debt, like credit card debt or a student loan
