Entertainment

These costs vary wildly based on your lifestyle choices.

Going Out

Whether it be with your significant other, your friends, or your family, you will probably spend part of your income on going out. It could be to eat, watch a movie, go to a sporting event, or any number of other things.

Television

Though it is becoming less common in households across the United States, cable television can be a significant expenses every month.

Subscription Services

Slowly replacing cable TV, streaming service subscriptions should be an expense you plan for. These can encompass movies, television, shows, and music. Some subscription plans are available with or without ads, changing the cost.
