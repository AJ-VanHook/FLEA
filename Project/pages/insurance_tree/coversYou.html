Insurance That Covers You

Health Insurance

Essential to support you through health complications, emergencies, sicknesses, and preventative care. Look for a low stop-loss/maximum out-of-pocket. This will save you more money in the event of a health event.

Dental Insurance

Covers routine cleaning and dental proceures.

Vision Insurance

Covers eye care, eye exams, and visual aids.

Disability Insurance

Helps you replace lost wages due to a injury or illness. Dave Ramsey's best practice is to have coverage for 65% of your current income.

Identity Theft Insurance

Up to 10 million Americans are victims of identity theft every year. This is why it is more and more important that you identitity theft protection. This type of insurance includes two main factors you should consider: credit report monitoring and restoration services. Credit report monitoring is exactly as it says. Restoration services actually take action when your identity may have been stolen, activley cleaning the mess made up for you.
