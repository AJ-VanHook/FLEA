What Constitues a Long Term Goal?

A long term goal is a goal which should take longer than 3 years to achieve. Financial long term goals typically change aspects of your life; perhaps you have more money to spend or maybe you now have a college degree.

Examples of Long Term Goals

  • Retirement saving
  • Put yourself through college
  • Buying a home
  • Becoming completely debt-free
